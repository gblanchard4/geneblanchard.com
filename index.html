Work Projects

I work as an Bioinformatics Analyst at LSU Health Sciences Center. I analyze sequenceing data that comes off the Illumina MiSeq.

Meet with the investigators

Discuss the project goals and aims. Find out what the investigator is looking for in the data.

Analyze the data

Use USEARCH to produce merge and filter reads, remove chimeras, and form a biom table. Move to Qiime for the secondary analysis which includes alpha diversity, taxa plots, beta diversity, core microbiome, and much more. I use my own scripts to tie a pipeline together and visualize the data in new ways.

Return the results

Generate html pages to make the results easier to navigate. Meet with the investigator to discuss the results. Dig deeper into the data and select new paths to hone down on.
